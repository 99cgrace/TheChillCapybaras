🌟 Glow and Flow

"show your glow, share your flow"

🎯 Pitch

Ever struggle with keeping a healthy schedule? What about academic pressure? Or maybe you just need someone to talk to? Look no further, "Glow and Flow" offers a 3-in-1 deal! This is the perfect app for teens!

🌍 Theme Implementation

The app that we plan on designing is a mental health awareness and it is supposed to target teens specifically. We work on studying schedules, planners, and check-ins

🧠 Why We Built This

As students ourselves, we know all too well the struggle of being a teen. We all have problems whether they stem from schoolwork, mental illness, or something at home. Our app is designed in a way where we ourselves would use it and hope that others will too!
